Once you’ve installed the module, you can see it in action instantly. For that, from your Odoo dashboard, head to your settings.

There, select Accounting, scroll down to the “Fiscal Country” section, and from there, select Armenia. By clicking the link, you’ll access more information about the target country.

In this section, as you scroll down, you can see the list of regions of Armenia with their respective state codes, as well as other information, including currency, country code, and calling code.

The module additionally includes Armenia’s chart of accounts. You can access it from the “Fiscal Localization” section at the top.

In the taxes section, you can see the default sales tax added automatically, as well as the Tax return periodicity, which you can set up manually.

By clicking on the link button next to Sales Tax, you'll go to the tax settings, where you can manually setup taxes when you need.

After the installation, the localization will automatically be added to all aspects of your Odoo account. Let’s look at the example of contacts.
You can access the list of cities and states in Armenia and select them. The cities are compatible with the regions, and once you select the city, the region will be auto-filled. In addition, the postal codes of cities and regions are displayed.

What is the Odoo Accounting Localization for Armenia module and why do I need it?

The module is an Odoo module designed to help you seamlessly and easily localize your Odoo eCommerce website Accounting for Armenia. If your eCommerce website does business in Armenia, the module can help you to easily run Odoo accounting for SMEs without any hassle.

What does the module include?

The module includes an Armenia-ready chart of accounts, a list of regions and Cities of Armenia with their respective state codes, and several other adaptations that you can access once you purchase the module. In the module, cities are compatible with regions, and information is often auto-filled to streamline setup processes.

Does the module include tax information?

Yes. Within the module, the default sales tax is added automatically. You can edit the information manually, as well as set up return periodicity.
